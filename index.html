About Us

Onyx Care offers semi-independent and supported accommodation to young people between the age 16-21. We support the most vulnerable young people in their time of need and ensure that they have the opportunity to thrive, no matter what the circumstances or background.

Predominantly, Onyx care supports young people who have been cared for by the Local Authority and are ready to or have to move out of residential care or foster homes. We also support separated migrant young people. We provide support and accommodation so that young people can eventually move on to independence in the timely, safe and planned way having been supported to overcome any challenges they have had in their lives.

We specialise in offering solo accommodation for the most vulnerable young people during their time of need and recognise that this particular group of vulnerable young people, require specialist support offered by the most resilient and creative workers who have a mandate to take a flexible approach to the support they provide.

Often the current circumstances and history may be a barrier to semi-independent providers offering accommodation to this specific group. Our approach is to ensure that we do not over stretch our resources by working with a small cohort of young people who can be given the specialist service they require.

Our staff team has a wealth of experience in Social Care and Youth Offending. As such, we can provide robust supervision and support in line with Court Orders, bail conditions and assessments of risk.

What we aim to achieve

Becoming independent.

While in our home, you’ll be helped to develop your independence. You will have access to cooking and washing facilities and everything else you need to help run your life. You’ll be able to live your life as a young adult. Working with your support workers, you will always have one eye on your final destination, independence. As you build independent living skills, we will help you to evidence your achievements and milestones so that when you want to move on to independence, you have the required evidence.

Reunite with family and friends

As you settle with us and leave the care system, you will have a home that is safe and secure as well as welcoming to those in your life. At that point you may wish to reunite with friends and family. Where appropriate, we will support you to do this in a safe way.

Gain employment/back to education.

Our team is here to help you with your education at any time. We will support you to fulfil the goals and objectives in your pathway plan and where necessary, to work with you and your Personal Advisor to set new goals. We will support you to prepare for interviews, visit college, make enquiries and much more.
