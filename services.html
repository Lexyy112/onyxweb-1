What we do

We support young people to make positive choices, and achieve their goals through the provision of supported accommodation, care, and support.

How we help young people

Supported Accomodation.

We provide support and accommodation so that young people can eventually move on to independence in the timely, safe and planned way having been supported to overcome any challenges they have had in their lives.

Counselling/Therapy

We offer counselling support using trusted outside therapists to help young people that are looked after, to overcome difficulties they have experienced , and to support them towards developing their self-confidence and self-esteem to live independent lives.

Solo Living.

We specialise in offering solo accommodation for the most vulnerable young people during their time of need and recognise that this particular group of vulnerable young people, require specialist support offered by the most resilient and creative workers who have a mandate to take a flexible approach to the support they provide.
